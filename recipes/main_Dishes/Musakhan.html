Musakhan

Musakhan is a traditional Palestinian dish made of roasted chicken baked with onions, sumac, allspice, saffron, and fried pine nuts, atop taboon bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons sumac
  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • Taboon bread or flatbread, for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in the sumac, allspice, salt, and pepper, and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. Place the chicken pieces in a baking dish and pour the onion mixture over them, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and golden brown.
  6. To serve, place the chicken and onions on the taboon bread and sprinkle with toasted pine nuts.
