Shawerma

Shawerma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made with thinly sliced meat, typically lamb, chicken, or beef, that is marinated and then cooked on a vertical rotisserie.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread, for serving
  • Chopped vegetables (tomato, cucumber, onion), for serving
  • Tahini sauce, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken thighs and toss to coat evenly. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the chicken onto skewers and grill or bake for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Remove the chicken from the skewers and slice thinly.
  6. Serve the shawerma in pita bread with chopped vegetables and tahini sauce.
